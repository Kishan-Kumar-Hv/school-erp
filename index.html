About Our Institution

Legacy of Faith, Knowledge, and Service in Hassan

The Holy Cross Sisterhood Legacy

Holy Cross Sisters School is managed by the Sisters of Mercy of the Holy Cross, a religious congregation founded in Switzerland in 1856 by Fr. Theodosius Florentini OFM Cap. and Mother Maria Theresa Scherer.

Our educators carry forward this rich legacy of compassion, social development, and high academic standards. Established in Hassan to serve the local community, the institution focus on raising ethical individuals who contribute positively to nation building.

We combine traditional values with 21st-century modern learning aids, ensuring a complete educational suite that prepares girls and boys for a dynamic future.

"In the cross of Christ is our strength. Through selfless service, we aim to bring the light of true education to every child." — Mother Maria Theresa Scherer

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that promotes intellectual maturation, ethical depth, and physical wellness. We aim to nurture socially conscious citizens who stand for truth, equality, and compassion.

Our Vision

To lead students towards academic brilliance and integral personality development, helping them recognize their unique, God-given potential to serve society with courage and integrity.

Core Values

  • Faith: Trust in divine providence.
  • Integrity: Honesty in thoughts and actions.
  • Equality: Equal respect for all backgrounds.
  • Service: Commitment to helping the needy.
